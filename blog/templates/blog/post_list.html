Review of “A View of Cloud Computing”

In 2010, cloud-conscious people presented 10 obstacles and opportunities from their perspective. 1.Availability/Business Continuity, Opportunity is Use Multiple Cloud Providers 2.Data Lock-In, Opportunity is Standardize APIs; Compatible SW to enable Surge or Hybird Cloud Computing 3.Data Confidentiality and Auditability, Opportunity is Deploy Encryption, VLANs, Firewalls 4.Data Transfer Bottlenecks, Opportunity is FedExing Disks; Higher BW Switches 5.Performance Unpredictability, Opportunity is Improved VM Support; Flash Memory; Gang Schedule VMs 6.Scalable Storage, Opportunity is Invent Scalable Store 7.Bugs in Large Distributed Systems, Opportunity is Invent Debugger that relies on Distributed VMs 8.Scaling Quickly, Opportunity is Invent Auto-Scaler that relies on ML; Snapshots for Conservation 9.Reputation Fate Sharing, Opportunity is Offer reputation-guarding services like those for email 10.Software Licensing, Opportunity is Pay-for-use licenses

Review of “Eventually consistent”

Werner Vogels summarizes five different scenarios for implementing Eventually Consistent for this ultimate consistency: ·Causal consistency ·Read-your-write consistency ·Session consistency ·Monotonic read consistency ·Monotonic write consistency In practice, these five designs are often used in combination to build a distributed system with ultimate consistency.
